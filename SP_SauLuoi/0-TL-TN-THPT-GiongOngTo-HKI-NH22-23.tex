	    	&=& \overrightarrow{AB} - \overrightarrow{AC} + \dfrac{1}{4}\overrightarrow{AB} + \dfrac{1}{4}\overrightarrow{AC} \\
	    	&=& \dfrac{5}{4}\overrightarrow{AB} - \dfrac{3}{4}\overrightarrow{AC}.
